Honeymoon Itinerary

✈ Travel
Day 1 May 31 · Departure

Depart LIC 1:30 PM → Newark → Air France

🏛 Florence
Day 2 June 1 · Arrive Florence

Land via Paris CDG. Check in: The Place Firenze Hotel. Lunch at Gustapizza. Walk around Florence.

Dinner at The Place + rooftop drinks.

Day 3 June 2 · Florence → Tuscany

Breakfast at The Place Firenze.

Medici Chapel. Uffizi Gallery + Vasari Corridor. Check out 4:00 PM → pick up rental car → drive to Sinalunga AirBnb.

Dinner at Ristorante Walter Redaelli.

🌿 Tuscany — Sinalunga
Day 4 June 3

Poliziano Winery tasting in Montepulciano. Drive through San Quirico d'Orcia. Dinner at Lupaia Restaurant.

Day 5 June 4 · Antinori Chianti

Antinori nel Chianti Classico — The Tinaia wine tour and lunch.

Hangout by the pool at AirBnb. Cook dinner.

Day 6 June 5

Olive oil tasting at Mascagni olive oil farm.

De'Ricci Winery tasting. Explore Montepulciano town.

Day 7 June 6 · Pool Day

Hangout by the pool. Dinner at Agriturismo Podere Spedalone.

Day 8 June 7 · Transfer Day

Leave Airbnb → drop rental car at Rome Fiumicino → air train to Roma Termini → train to Salerno → private driver to Monastero Santa Rosa. Dinner at il Refettorio.

⛵ Amalfi Coast
Day 9 June 8 · Monastero Santa Rosa

Explore Amalfi. Spa/massages at the hotel. Dinner at il Refettorio.

Day 10 June 9 · Move to Praiano

Pool day. Check out of hotel and move to Praiano Airbnb.

Path of the gods hike from Praiano. Cook dinner. Drinks at Café Mirante.

Day 11 June 10 · Boat Day

Full day boat charter along the Amalfi Coast. Lunch in Nerano (il Cantuccio). Dinner at Casa Angelina.

Day 12 June 11

Water taxi to Positano. Walk around and beach club at Da Ferdinando. Dinner at Il Pirata.

Day 13 June 12

Beach club at La Gavitella. Cooking class at Sergio's Cooking Experience.

Day 14 June 13 · Departure

Naples → Dublin → NYC.
